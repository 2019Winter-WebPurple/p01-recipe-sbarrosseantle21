This is the thing I eat every single morning. It's an original tried and true classic, and I hope you enjoy it.

Things you will need:

  • An alarm clock
  • A Kuerig from 2008 that kind of doesn't work.
  • A lack of motivation
  • A loving mother who will buy you cheap granola bars from Costco in bulk.

Directions:

  1. Set your alarm clock way too early, because you actually want to cook something to get your day started right.
  2. Press snooze a minimum of three times.
  3. Wake up in a panic, and ONLY brush your teeth and wash your face. If you have time for makeup and hair, then you didn't press snooze enough times.
  4. Stumble into the kitchen in a sleepy haze, and make yourself a large coffee in a to-go mug. Then, stand around the kitchen while you wait five minutes for your (sightly broken) Kuerig to make your coffee.
  5. Realize that making coffee now made you late (its worth it, I'm a monster when I am uncaffeinated), and grab a Kirkland brand granola bar from the pantry and sprint out the door without a coat.

Prep-time: 5-10 minutes.

This recipe is perfect for especially weekdays, but can definatly be served on the weekends. It is commonly acompanied by minor sleep deprivation and a hatred for mornings/waking up.
